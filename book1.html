Book Title:DO YOU WONDER HOW SNAKES SLITHER?

Author:

Axel Scheffler

Story:

There is a chatty snake in the garden and its on a monologue. Would you stop and listen or would you rather run? But before you decide, know this for sure..that this snake is no ordinary, he is quite the charmer.

Moral:

Appearances can be deceiving—sometimes it's worth listening before you judge.
